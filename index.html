Sam Kahler is a writer, marketer, and digital strategist.

I tell stories and create campaigns that invite reflection and exploration. I'm currently a content marketer at Uniswap Labs, serving its mission to unlock value through universal exchange.

Past roles include: (1) Leading SEO & content strategy at The Giving Block, a crypto philanthropy company that serves donors and nonprofits. (2) Directing communications for Asymptote Journal, which helps introduce hidden gems of translated literature to the masses. (3) Digital marketing at AFS Intercultural Programs, a global student exchange program provider.

Once upon a time I also moonlighted as a playwright. Select works had staged performances, workshops and readings in venues like Littlefield, Theater 80, Jersey City Theater Center, Pete’s Candy Bar, the Cherry Lane Theatre, and The Hive (RIP).

I'm a graduate of Skidmore College, with a B.A. in English. If you get to know me, you'll find me to be surprisingly lax about Oxford comma usage.

I live in Brooklyn, New York, where I enjoy biking over the Gowanus Canal daily.
